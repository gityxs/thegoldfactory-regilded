The Gold Factory Regilded Laboratory Guide

Here, you can see a full list of potions with their effects and recipes.
Protip: You can make multiple potions at once by multiplying the quantity of all ingredients!

Empty Potion

Effect: Nothing! Yes, nothing!
How to make: Mix 1 health potion and 100 gold bars.

Poison Potion

Effect: Deals instant damage to the enemy
How to make: Mix 1 empty potion, 1 health potion and 100 iron bars.

Confusion Potion

Effect: Makes the enemy attack themselves one time
How to make: Mix 1 poison potion and 200 gold bars.

Invisibility Potion

Effect: Makes you invisible and lets you see invisible objects.
How to make: Mix 1 empty potion and 500 iron bars.

Instant Countdown Potion

Effect: All running countdowns are finished instantly! (except for the potion countdown)
How to make: Mix 1 empty potion, 20 cookies and 2000 gold bars

Suicide Potion

Effect: Kills yourself instantly...?
How to make: Mix 1 empty potion, 1 poison potion and 1 confusion potion

Cookie Potion

Effect: Unknown :o
How to make: Mix 1 empty potion and 500 cookies

X Potion (not the Candy Box one)

Effect: They told me not to drink this potion.
How to make: Mix all potions listed above (1 of each potion), then add 100,000 gold bars and 100,000 iron bars. Enjoy?
Notice:
- Please add the ingredients in order!
- For the health potion, you can't make it, you must buy it at the shop :p
- Also, the health potion is treated separately from these potions :D
- Once you put items in, you can't take them out anymore, so be careful!
